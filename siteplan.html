Website Plan

Site Name

EcoTravel Adventures

This name represents a website focused on eco-friendly travel adventures and tips. The site aims to promote sustainable tourism practices.

Site Purpose

The site provides information on eco-friendly travel destinations, tips for sustainable travel, and a community forum for eco-travel enthusiasts.

Scenarios

  • What are the best eco-friendly travel destinations in Europe?
  • How can I minimize my carbon footprint while traveling?

  • Light Goldenrod Yellow (Background)

Typography

  • Open Sans - Used for body text
